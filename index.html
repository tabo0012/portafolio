Something about me

David Alejandro Taborda Castro, a 30-year-old Colombian journalist, possesses an enduring passion for photography that was nurtured during his university years. In his role as a committed storyteller, he embarked on a professional journey within the media landscape, eventually discovering his niche in the enthralling world of photojournalism. Guided by his unwavering dedication to skill refinement, David pursued an extensive photography course that not only broadened his perspective but also heightened his capacity to capture the fleeting essence of life's moments.David's trajectory led him to diverse platforms, where he notably excelled during his tenure at a prominent Colombian TV news organization.

With a camera as his instrument and a journalist's inquisitiveness as his driving impetus, he has relentlessly striven to unveil the concealed narratives that mold our world. With each snap of the shutter, David endeavors to encapsulate more than just visuals—his aim is to encapsulate emotions, truths, and the profound core of the human experience. His unwavering dedication to journalism coupled with his deep affection for photography fuels his aspiration to continually illuminate multifaceted narratives.
